Overview

This page documents the functionality of the Patrik Product Sync, which synchronizes products from Time 4 Action warehouse to the Creaglobe system (Metakocka). It is designed to automate stock updates, scheduling, and log tracking.

Key Features

  • Automated sync at configurable intervals (15 min, hourly, 3 hours, daily).
  • Selective syncing based on day of the week.
  • Real-time logs of sync operations with timestamp and stock link.

Cron Expressions

Supported intervals:

Interval Cron Example Description
Every 15 minutes 0-59/15 * * * 1,2,3 Syncs every 15 minutes on Mon, Tue, Wed
Hourly 0 2-23/1 * * * Syncs every hour starting 2 AM
Every 3 hours 0 2-23/3 * * * Syncs every 3 hours starting 2 AM
Daily 0 2 * * * Syncs once daily at 2 AM

Logs

The system keeps track of the latest sync runs, including timestamp, sync name, and a link to the stock list.
